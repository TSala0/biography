Experience

I did L3 computer science course in college which was 2 years long. Within them 2 years the technology we were learning varied, from learning how to create a network using cisco packet tracer to understanding how to protect a computer from malicious software and how to deter criminals from targeting a PC.
I also learnt and understood how project management worked and how to effectively use a Gantt chart to schedule time which helps with time management skills
